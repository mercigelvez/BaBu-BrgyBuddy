About Babu

Babu is your digital Barangay Document Assistant, designed to make the process of document inquiries and applications easy. We're here to bridge the gap between residents and the barangay administration.

With Babu, you can easily access information about various barangay documents, check application requirements, and schedule your request - all from the convenience of your device.

We're committed to enhancing the efficiency of barangay services and supporting our community. Babu offers a range of features to help you:

  • Inquire about available barangay documents
  • Check document requirements and fees
  • Schedule appointments for document processing
  • Stay updated on barangay announcements related to document services

Meet Babu - Your Brgy. Document Inquiry AI Assistant

Official Partnership

This AI chatbot is officially partnered with the Barangay Market Area of Santa Rosa, Laguna.

Our collaboration ensures that you receive accurate, up-to-date information about barangay documents and services specific to our locality. Babu is here to serve the residents of Brgy. Market Area, Santa Rosa with the support and approval of the barangay officials.
